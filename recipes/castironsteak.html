Cast Iron Steak

Description

Recipe for a steak made in a cast iron pan. Seering the steak intially in the cast iron pan and then finishing off in the oven is a great way to cook the steak to your liking. Season with garnishments of your choice, below are a few we recommend.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Steak (12 Ounce)
  • Fresh Rosemary
  • Freshly Ground Salt and Pepper
  • 2 teaspoons Olive Oil
  • Freshly chopped Garlic
  • Fresh Thyme

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Remove casserole dish from refrigerator. Cover steaks with plastic wrap and let allow to come to room temperature, at least 15 minutes.
  2. Place steaks on a clean work surface and generously rub with steak seasoning and black pepper.
  3. Flip and cook until browned on the other side and red in the center, about 2 minutes more. Transfer skillet with the steaks to the oven.
